Fresh Raspberry Coupe

Ingredients:

1-1/2 cups fresh raspberries, cleaned

2 tbsp orange liqueur

1 cup Pastry Cream for fruit tarts

1-1/2 cups whioed cream

Directions:

Divide half of raspberries between 4 dessert glasses. Sprinkle each with half of liqueur.

Add thi layer of pastry cream and thin layer of whipped cream.

Continue Layering ingredients in some order until used. Refrigerate 1 hr. before serving.
