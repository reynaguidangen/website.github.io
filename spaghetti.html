Spaghetti With White Wine Meat Sauce

Ingredients:

2 tbsp olive oil

1 garlic clove, smashed and chopped

1 medium onion, chopped

1 celery stalk, diced

1 lb lean ground beef

1 cup dry white wine

2 1/2 tomato sauce

1/2 tsp basil

1/2 thyme

1 tbsp chopped fresh parsely

4 portion cooked spaghetti, hot

salt and pepper

Directions:

*Heat oil saute pan over medium heat. Add garlic, onion and celery. Cook 3 minutes over medium heat.

*Add meat. Season with salt and pepper. Mix and cook 6 minutes over low heat.

*Pour in wine, stir and cook 4 minutes.

*Incorporate tomato sauce and seasonings. Mix and cook 6 minutes over low heat.

Serve over hot spaghetti.
