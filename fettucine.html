Fettuccine in cream

Ingrdients:

1 tbsp butter

1/2 tsp white pepper

1/3 cayenne pepper

1/2 tsp oregano

1/2 tsp celery seed

2 garlic cloves, smashed and chopped

1 onion, finely chopped

2 cups light cream

1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese

4 portions cooked spinach fettucine, hot

pinch nutmeg

salt and pepper

Directions:

*Heat butter in saucepan over medium heat. Add seasonings, garlic and onion. Mix and cook 4 minutes over low heat.

*Incorporate cream and bring to boil over medium heat. Stir and continue cooking 8 minutes over low heat.

*Stir in cheese and simmer 3 minutes.

*Add hot cooked pasta. mix well and simmer several minutes. (Use fresh pasta if available). Serve
