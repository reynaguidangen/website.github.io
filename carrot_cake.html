Carrot Cake

Ingredients:

1-1/2 cups granulated sugar

1/2 cup brown sugar

1-1/2 vegetable oil

2 cups all-purpose-flour, sifted

1-1/2 tsp baking powder

1 tsp cinnamon

1 tsp baking soda

1 tsp salt

5 large eggs

2-1/2 cups grated carrots

1 cup golden raisins

1 tbsp flour

1 tbsp grated lemon rind

Directions:

*Preheat oven to 350 F(180 C). Butter sides and bottom of 9-1/2-in springform cake pan with depth of 2-1/2-in. set aside.

*Place both sugars and oil in bowl. Whisk together.

In second bowl, sift in flour, baking powder, cinnamon, baking soda and salt. incorporate dry ingredients into sugar mixture using electric hand mixer

*Add eggs, one at the time, mixing well between each addition. Use electric hand mixer.

*Incorporate carrots. Dust raisins with 1tbsp flour and stir into batter. Mix in lemon rind

*Pour batter into cake pan and bake 60-70 minutes.

*When cake is done, remove from oven and let stand in mold 5 minutes. Unmold and finish cooling on wire rack.

*Serve plain or with icing.
