Sunnyside Up Eggs on Tomatoes

Ingrdients:

2 tbsp olive oil

2 toamtoes, peeled and diced

1 small garlic clove, smashed and chopped

1 tsp chopped fresh parsely

4 large eggs

4 pinch oregano

salt and pepper

Directions:

*Heat half of oil in nonstick frying pan over high heat. Add tomatoes, garlic, parsely and oregano; season well. Cook 7-8 minutes over medium heat. Transfer mixture to heated platter and set aside, keeping hot.

*Add remaining oil to frying pan over medium-high heat. Break in eggs and season. Cover and cook 3-4 minutes until whites are set.

*Slide eggs out onto hot tomato mixture and serve.
