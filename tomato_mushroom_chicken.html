Tomato Mushroom Chicken

Ingrdients:

1/2 cup flour

3 1/2 lbs chicken, cleaned and cut into 8 pieces

2 tbsp butter

1 lb fresh mushrooms, cleaned and halved

1 tbsp oil

1 large onion, diced large

28-oz can tomatoes, drained and coarsely chopped

1 tbsp curry powder

1/2 oregano

salt and pepper

paprika

few drops tabasco sauce

Directions:

Mix flour with salt, pepper and paprika; dredge chicken pieces in flour mixture.

Heat butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add chicken and cook 15 minutes, turning pieces over twice.

Add mushrooms, seasons and continue cooking 10-12 minutes

Meanwhile, heat oil in frying pan over high heat. Add onion and cook 4 mintes. Add tomatoes and all seasonings including tabasco; cook 8-10 minutes over medium heat.

Spoon mixture over chicken and serve.
