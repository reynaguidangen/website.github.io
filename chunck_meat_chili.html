Chunk Meat Chili

Ingrdients:

Part 1;

2 1/2 cups dried kidney beans

2 1/2 boneless blade steak, cut in 1/2-in(1.2cm) cubes

1 celery stalk

1 onion, cubed

1 bay leaf

2 cloves

salt and pepper

Part 2;

1/4 tsp ground cloves

1/2 tsp celery seeds

1 basil

1/2 tsp cumin

2 1/2 tbsp chili powder

1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

1/4 tsp thyme

1 tbsp oregano

1 tsp black pepper

1/2 jalapeno pepper, chopped

1 1/2 tbsp olive oil

1 large onion, chopped

3 garlic cloves, smashed and chopped

3 carrots, pared and sliced

1 yellow pepper, cubed medium

2 tbsp flour

28-oz can toamatoes, drained and chopped

2 cups beef stock, heated

salt

Directions:

Part 1;

*Soak beans in cold water 8 hrs. Drain and set aside.

*Place meat and drained beans in large saucepan. Pour in enough cold water to cover ingrediemts by 1-in. Bring to boil over high heat. Skim off foam.

*Drain meat and beans; retuen to saucepan. Pour in enough cold water to cover ingredients by 1-in(2.5cm).Add celery, onion,bay leaf and cloves. Season well. Partly cover and bring to boil. Cook 1 1/2 hrs. over medium heat.

Part 2;

*Mix spices, herbs and jalapeno pepper together; set aside

*Heat oil in large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion ans garlic; cook 3 minutes over low heat. Add carrots and peppers; mix well.

Stir in seasoning mixture. Mix very well (important). Cook 5 minutes over medium heat.Sprinkle flour over vegestable, stir well and cook 3 minutes over a low heat.

*Add tomatoes and beef stock; correct seasoning with salt. Mix and cook 40 minutes, partly covered, over low heat.

*When meat is cooked, drain bean and meat mixture, reserving 1/2 cup of the cooking liquid.

*Add meat and beans to vegetables mixture. Add reserved cooking liquid and mix well. Partly cover and cook 20 minutes over low heat. If desired, serve with sour cream and cheddar
