Pasta with Spring Vegetable

Ingredients:

1 tbsp olive oil

3 green onions, chopped

2 carrots, pared and sliced on the bias

1 tbsp chopped fresh ginger

1/2 tsp basil

1 green pepper, diced

1/2 cup dry white wine

1 1/2 cups chicken stock, heated

1 head broccoli, washed and in florets

1 tbsp cornstarch

3 tbsp cold water

4 portions cooked pasta, hot

salt and pepper

Directions:

Heat oil in frying pan over medium heat. Add green onions and carrots; cook 3 minutes

Add ginger, basil, green pepper and wine. Mix and cook 4 minutes

Add chicken stock and broccoli. Season and cook 4 minutes

Dissolve cornstarch in cold water, incorporate into sauce and cook 1 minute. Correct seasoning.

Serve over pasta.
