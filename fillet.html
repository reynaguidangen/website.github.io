Fillet with Flounder with Oyster sauce

Ingrdients:

1/2 tsp white pepper

1/2 tsp black pepper

1/4 tsp dry mustard

1/4 cayenne pepper

1/2 tsp celery seed

1/4 ground genger

1 cup flour

4 flounder filets

1 cup milk

3 tbsp butter

1 1/2 oyster sauce, heated

Directions:

Place spices in mortar and grind together with pestle. Mix flour with spices.

Dip filets in milk, then dredge in spiced flour.

Heat butter in cast iron pan over medium heat. Add filets and cook 3 minutes on each side. Remove fish and serve with oyster sauce.
