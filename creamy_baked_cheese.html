Fresh Raspberry Coupe

Ingredients:

Crumb Crust;

1 cup graham cracker crumbs

1/4 cup granulated sugar

1/4 cup melted butter

Cake;

3 1/2-lb packages cream cheese, at room temperature

1-1/2 cups granulated sugar

1 light cream

1 tbsp vanilla

5 tbsp all-purpose-cream

2 large eggs

Directions:

*Pre-heat oven 350 F. Combine graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 sugar and melted butter. Press into bottom of buttered 9-1/2-in springform cake pan, with a depth of 2-1/2-in.

*Bake crumb crust 10 minutes. Removed and set aside.

Place cream cheese in bowl of electric mixer and beat until creamy.Add sugar and beat to incorporate.

*Add light cream, vanilla and flour. Beat until incorporated. Scrape down sides of bowl as needed.

*Add eggs and beat once more. Pour batter into prepared cake pan. Bake 55 minutes

*When cheesecake is cooked, removed from the oven and let stand on counter to cool. Refrigerate atleast 8 hrs. before slicing. Serve plain or with sliced fruit.
